    LazyProf

    Lazy Prof is an innovative feature designed to simplify academic planning and resource management for students. It allows students to streamline study habits by aggregating critical course information, lecture summaries, study materials, and assignment due dates from professors and other classmates. By centralizing this data, Lazy Prof helps students access key resources and reminders in one place, reducing the need for manual tracking across multiple sources. This feature aims to improve study efficiency and make academic management less time-consuming, allowing students to focus on learning rather than organizing.
